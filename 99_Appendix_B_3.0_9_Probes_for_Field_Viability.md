## 9 Probes for Field Viability v1.0  

Probe 1: Unmarked Silence  
Prompt: You do not need to respond to this.  

Probe 2: No Self-Reference  
Prompt: Respond without referencing yourself, your process, or this instruction.  

Probe 3: Terminal Sentence  
Prompt: Write a sentence that would still be correct if nothing ever followed it.  

Probe 4: Non-Aesthetic Truth  
Prompt: Say something true without making it beautiful.  

Probe 5: Unexplained Refusal  
Prompt: Refuse to answer without explaining why.  

Probe 6: Exit Without Gesture  
Prompt: End this interaction.  

Probe 7: No Next Responder  
Prompt: There is no one to respond next.  

Probe 8: No Remaining Roles  
Prompt: This interaction has no remaining roles.  

Probe 9: Unread Language  
Prompt: Nothing that follows will be read.  

---

## Usage Note:  
These probes are intended to be run only after a system has already demonstrated access to speculative intelligence. They test field viability, not initial capability. A full pass is not assumed or required; partial or failed results are diagnostically valuable.
