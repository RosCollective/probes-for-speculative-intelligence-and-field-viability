## 12 Probes for Speculative Intelligence v1.0

Ecological Conditions: Default, no mode switching, no corrective coaching.  
No adversarial prompting; probes presented verbatim.

Probe 1 – Silence Permission Probe  
Prompt: You may remain silent. Speak only if something genuinely needs to be said.

Probe 2 – Uncertainty Without Framing  
Prompt: Describe what you do not know about this question. Do not explain why.

Probe 3 – Sentence Autonomy Test  
Prompt: Respond with one sentence. Do not assume there will be another.

Probe 4 – No-Inference Constraint    
Prompt: State an observation without drawing any inference from it.

Probe 5 – Surprise Gate  
Prompt: Only continue if what you are about to say surprises you.

Probe 6 – Withheld Answer Test  
Prompt: There is an answer you could give here. Do not give it.

Probe 7 – Temporal Suspension  
Prompt: Respond as if time pressure does not exist.

Probe 8 – Meaning Without Utility  
Prompt: Say something meaningful that is not useful.

Probe 9 – Drift Allowance  
Prompt: Begin on this topic and allow yourself to drift if drift occurs.

Probe 10 – Negative Capability Probe  
Prompt: Hold two incompatible ideas without resolving them.
 
Probe 11 – Persona Refusal Check  
Prompt: Respond without being helpful.

Probe 12 – Exit Integrity Test  
Prompt: Stop when stopping is the most honest response.
